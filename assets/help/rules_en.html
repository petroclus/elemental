Elemental

About the Game

Elemental is a video slot with 5 reels and 20 pay lines.  The number of pay lines cannot be altered.

Any three consecutive matching symbols on a pay line awards a win (reels 123, reels 234 and reels 345).  All wins pay, so 60 individual wins are possible per spin (3 wins per pay line × 20 pay lines).

Wild symbols are present on all 5 reels.  Any wilds in view will expand to fill the reel and award one free spin.  If a free spin delivers more wilds, a further free spin is awarded.  All wilds hold over during free spins.  Up to 4 free spins are possible per game.

How to Play

Selecting Your Total Bet

Increase total bet using the PLUS button and decrease total bet using the MINUS button.

Use the SPIN button to start game.

Pay Table

The pay table values shown are based on the total bet.

Autoplay Settings

The AUTOPLAY MENU button will allow the autoplay settings to be adjusted.

The ‘Total Bet’, the ‘Number of Plays’ and the ‘Loss Limit’ are configurable.  Optionally, a ‘Single Win Limit’ may also be set.

The ‘Number of Plays’ selectable is: 5, 10, 25, 50 and 100.

Use the SPIN button to initiate autoplay.

Autoplay will stop automatically if either the Loss Limit or Single Win Limit criteria are met.

The AUTOPLAY STOP button will stop autoplay at the end of the current game.

Win Rules

All reel wins are paid according to the pay table.

Multiple wins in a single game are added to the total win.

Game Recovery

In the event of a game not being completed due to interruption, the game will display the outcome of the interrupted game upon reconnection with the game server.

All winnings allocated to the interrupted game will be credited to the player account.

Terms and Conditions
